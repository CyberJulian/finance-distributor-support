Finance Distributor

Privacy Policy & Support — Last updated May 28, 2026

Privacy Policy

Finance Distributor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter — paycheck amounts, categories, and history — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage and is never shared with any third party.

The app has no account system, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no network requests of any kind. Your financial data never leaves your device.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.
